***LOGARITMO***
  ->log(n) = logaritmo natural
  ->log10(n) = logaritmo na base 10

*Propriedades
1. logc(a*b) = logc(a) + logc(b)  |  (logc == logaritmo na base c)
2. logc(a/b) = logc(a) - logc(b)  |   (logc == logaritmo na base c)
3. logb(a) = logc(a)/logc(b)
4. lob(a^n) = n*log(a)
